Introduction

About Me

Hi there! I’m Simpson, a cybersecurity enthusiast with a deep passion for digital security within the technology world. Workwise, currently, I'm a part of a pilot program with Roche called KL IT Graduates Program!! While joining the graduate program allowed me to learn all kinds of practical skills as an Information Security Analyst in real life scenarios, I’m currently also pursuing my master’s degree in cybersecurity with University Malaya here in Malaysia, which gave me the opportunity to dive even deeper into the field and hone my cybersecurity skills.

One of my favourite ways to sharpen my knowledge is by tinkering with Capture the Flag (CTF) online, where I enjoy solving challenges and testing my abilities in different real-world scenarios. Although I'm still a beginner at it, I like to think that it is a fun way to stay up-to-date with the latest security trends and techniques, so I often stay consistent on platforms such as TryHackMe and HackTheBox. When I’m not exploring vulnerabilities or cracking codes, I enjoy a more laid-back hobby: sleeping! It’s a great hobby.. you should try too :)

    My Career Objectives

    As a passionate and motivated cybersecurity enthusiast, I am excited to begin my journey in Blue Team roles within the cybersecurity industry. My current objective is to build a solid foundational knowledge through continuous learning and hands-on experience, with a focus on mastering key areas such as threat detection and network security. Rather than immediately pursuing higher-level positions, I aim to apply my skills in real-world scenarios to strengthen my expertise and work towards obtaining industry-recognized certifications that will support my career growth with the end goal of becoming a Threat Analyst.

    Current Goals
    • Achieve CompTIA Security+ Certification
    • Earn BTL1 Certification
    • Secure a career as a Threat Analyst and gain practical experience
    • Attain BTL2 Certification
    • Pursue CISSP Certification
